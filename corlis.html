CORLIS' PURGA

Over twenty years ago, in the year 2000, I wrote a book about a fragment of a lost poem known as the 'Purga' by the author Corlis. The bulk of the book is taken up with a commentary on the poem.

It was all inspired by several years of college spent seeped in Ancient Greek tragedies and the acres of print explicating every tiny detail of their texts. It proved a very useful format for collecting all the random ideas bottling about in my head at the time.
